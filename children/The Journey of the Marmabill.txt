6- The Croc-Catcher by Joff Dyer

The busy clerk, Jess, is the only person who seems to have noticed that no crocodiles have been swimming into Wasted Bay. The Croc-Catcher has done his duty, but it’s up to Jess to solve the mystery.

This is a delightfully quirky board book for younger readers. Although not written by Martin Lewis, as the title might suggest, it has received awards and acclaim from children’s book critics around the world. The story is original and dynamic, and is chock full of colourful characters. It’s a gentle story of perseverance that can be enjoyed by readers of all ages.
